\begin{align*}
\int p(x)dx&=\sum_{i=1}^{n}c_i\frac{1}{i}x^{i}\\
\int_{\alpha}^\beta&=\sum_{i=1}^{n}c_i\frac{1}{i}(\beta^i-\alpha^i)
\end{align*}
therefore we have
$$a=\boxed{\left(c_1(\beta-\alpha)),...,\frac{c_n}{n}(\beta^n-\alpha^n)\right)}$$

thus we get
